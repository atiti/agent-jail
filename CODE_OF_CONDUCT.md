# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, technically grounded collaboration.

## Our standards

- Be direct and constructive.
- Prefer evidence over rhetoric.
- Keep disagreements technical, not personal.
- Respect maintainers' time by bringing clear reproduction steps and concrete proposals.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Deliberate disclosure of private credentials or sensitive operator data
- Spam, trolling, or low-effort issue dumping

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, close discussions, or block participation when behavior violates these standards.
